ClosetIQ Privacy Policy

Your wardrobe data stays yours

Effective: January 1, 2025

ClosetIQ ("we," "us," "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have. We keep it simple and honest.

📦 What We Collect

We collect only what's needed to run the app:

  • Account info — email address (if you sign up with email or Apple), or an anonymous user ID if you skip sign-up.
  • Wardrobe data — clothing item names, categories, colors, wear counts, and formality tags you enter.
  • Photos — photos you upload of your clothing, stored securely in cloud storage. You can delete them at any time.
  • Location (approximate) — used only to show you local weather for outfit suggestions. We never store your location.
  • Usage data — basic app analytics (crashes, feature usage) to help us improve the app. No personal identifiers are attached.

🤖 AI & Photo Processing

When you use AI features (auto-categorize, outfit suggestions), your clothing photos or descriptions are sent to our secure AI backend. Photos are processed to identify the item, color, and category — and are not used to train AI models or shared with third parties beyond our processing infrastructure.

📸 Photos are compressed and sent over HTTPS. We do not store raw photos longer than needed to complete the analysis.

☁️ How We Store Your Data

Wardrobe items and outfit plans are stored in Supabase, a secure PostgreSQL cloud database hosted in the United States. All data is encrypted in transit (TLS) and at rest (AES-256).

Each user's data is isolated — other users cannot access your wardrobe, and we employ row-level security policies to enforce this at the database level.

🔒 How We Use Your Data

  • To provide outfit recommendations and weekly planning
  • To sync your wardrobe across devices
  • To send opt-in morning outfit reminders (notifications)
  • To process purchases and manage your subscription status
  • To improve app reliability and fix bugs

We do not sell your data to advertisers or third parties. Ever.

🔔 Push Notifications

If you enable morning reminders, we send a daily notification to prompt you to log your outfit. You can turn this off at any time in Settings → Notifications → ClosetIQ or within the app under Profile.

💳 Subscriptions & Purchases

Payments are handled by Apple In-App Purchase and RevenueCat. We never see or store your payment card details. RevenueCat receives a anonymized customer identifier to track subscription status. See RevenueCat's privacy policy at revenuecat.com/privacy.

🧑‍⚖️ Your Rights

  • Access — request a copy of all data we hold about you
  • Deletion — delete your account and all associated data from within the app (Profile → Delete Account) or by emailing us
  • Portability — export your wardrobe data as a structured file on request
  • Correction — update or correct your data at any time in-app
  • Opt-out — turn off notifications or analytics sharing at any time

Residents of the EU/EEA have additional rights under GDPR. California residents have rights under CCPA. Contact us to exercise any of these rights.

👶 Children's Privacy

ClosetIQ is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

🔄 Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. When we make material changes, we'll notify you in-app and update the "Effective" date at the top. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance.
